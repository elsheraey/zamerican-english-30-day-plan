---

## Dear Friend,

You've already crushed Level 1. You know your alphabet, your basic grammar, your vocabulary foundations. That part is behind you now.

This book is your next 30 days. It's not a suggestion — it's a commitment. Every day has a clear mission: watch the videos, do your Duolingo, and show up again tomorrow. No skipping. No "I'll do two tomorrow." Today's work is today's work.

By Day 30, you will think in English. You will hear English and understand it. You will speak and people will listen.

That's it. One hour a day for 30 days, and you will be a different English speaker.
